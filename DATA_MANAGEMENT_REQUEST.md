# Data Management Request Instructions

**Last Updated:** January 2025

To manage your data, send an email to [clutch3.info@gmail.com](mailto:clutch3.info@gmail.com) with your request.

## Delete All Data

Send an email to clutch3.info@gmail.com requesting to delete all your personal data. Include your account email address.

## Partial Deletion

Send an email to clutch3.info@gmail.com requesting partial deletion. Specify which data you want deleted (e.g., videos, statistics, profile information) and include your account email address.

## Request Your Data

Send an email to clutch3.info@gmail.com requesting a copy of your personal data. Include your account email address.

---

All requests will be processed within 30 days.
